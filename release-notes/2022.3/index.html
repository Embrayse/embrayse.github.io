What’s new in release 2022.3

This is our third major release of Embrayse for 2022 and we have been hard at work to bring you recipes and nutritional information. Let's take a peek:

Recipes

You asked and we delivered! You may now optionally record your recipes directly in Embrayse and do away with spreadsheets and paper. We have included a large database for ingredients, sourced from Food Standards Australia New Zealand (FSANZ) with common weight and volume based units of measure. If you can't find what you're looking for, you can always create custom ingredients.

Nutrition Facts

As a tool to help you review the nutritional value of each meal, we have built a nutrition fact calculator which uses the ingredients in your recipe to produce an estimate of relevant energy, macro and micro nutrients present per serving.

Nutrition facts require the use of pre-defined ingredients from Embrayse which include full nutritional info. If you are using custom ingredients, be aware that they will not be included in the nutrition calculation. We are continually reviewing new ingredients used by you and will expand the database to include these.

Recipe Viewer

Recipes are only as good as how easily they can be accessed by your staff. Your team can now quickly view recipes for any meals that have them, on their tablets. It's easy to see the instructions from a distance using the fullscreen and zoom features.

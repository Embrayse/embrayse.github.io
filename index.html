It's Time For a Change

If you are responsible for resident meals at an aged care home, and frustrated by incomplete and cumbersome processes and systems to manage food services; if you are wondering whether you have all the right tools to give your residents a great catering experience and empower your team to do the same, then we are here to help!

What We Offer

Let us take care of the mundane tasks so that you can focus on the important things.

Personalised Service

Sue likes her tea with two sugars and almond milk after dinner? Too easy. Embrayse makes it simple to manage complex resident preferences without relying on memory or paper.

From Order to Delivery

Embrayse helps you manage your entire process from meal orders, to production and plating, to delivery, so you save valuable time and resources.

Safe Eating

Our smart menu filtering and alert systems give you confidence that residents can only choose meals that match their dietary needs, like allergies.

Happy Staff

Delight your team by providing them with software that is simple, intuitive, and a joy to use, with little to no training needed. Happy staff means happy residents!

Family Portal

Differentiate yourself in the market by providing unique services, such as self-service and family portal for meal ordering and viewing meal history.

Forecasting

Embrayse lets you capture orders multiple days in advance and view aggregated reports, so that you make smarter food planning and purchasing decisions.

Are You Ready To Get Started?

Our process is simple. Get in touch with us to book a free consultation, where we will go over your operations and needs and figure out together if Embrayse is right for you. We'll then set you up with an appropriate plan that fits your needs, and walk you through everything you need to get up and running and achieve a successful outcome.

Why Choose Us

We understand how complex and challenging food management at aged care homes can be, and how much of a risk it is if you get it wrong. We strive to help you eliminate cumbersome manual tasks so that you can focus on the right problems.

Simplicity

User experience means everything to us. We build software that are intuitive, simple to use, and a delight for your team and your residents.

Expertise

We have extensive experience in developing Human Centred digital solutions for many industries, and have expertise working with aged care providers across Australia.

Reliable Team

We have a long term vision and an agile approach. We work closely with you to identify challenges and come up with the right solutions.

Customer Success

Your success is our main goal. We'll work with you, train your team, and provide ongoing support to make sure you get tangible value from your investment.

Data Privacy

We work hard to ensure all your data is stored securely and privately, and that you are compliant with Australian data privacy laws.

Innovation

More and more, technology is becoming a key component in providing great services. We strive to help you stay at the forefront of digital transformation in aged care.
